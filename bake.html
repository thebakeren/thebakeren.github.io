    Tyre and Dye Cup Cake

    Take a trip back to the sixties with these sweetly psychedelic cupcakes. Each is a simple white cake, but tinting the batter all the colors of the rainbow makes them funky and fun!— Gwyn dolyn Wilkerson, Kyle, Texas.

    Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 350°. In a large bowl, combine cake mix and soda.
    2. Beat on low speed 30 seconds. Beat on medium 1 minute.
    3. Divide batter among five bowls. Add a few drops of food coloring to each bowl;
    4. stir just until combined.
    5. Drop a spoonful of one color of batter into each of 18 paper-lined muffin cups.
    6. Layer with remaining colored batter until muffin cups are two-thirds full.
    7. Bake 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.
    8. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to cool completely.
    9. Frost tops and decorate with sprinkles. Yield: 1-1/2 dozen.
